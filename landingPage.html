Welcome to AstonCV!

What is AstonCV?

  • AstonCV is a platform designed to help students create and manage their CVs effectively.
  • You can show off your skills and experience in a professional way.
  • Your CVs can be used to apply for jobs and internships.

Why use AstonCV?

  • It provides a user-friendly interface for creating and editing CVs.
  • You can get feedback from fellow Aston University students and mentors.
  • You can also provide feedback to other students to help them improve their CVs.

Get started today!

  • Click on the "Register" button to create your account.
  • Once registered, you can start building your CV by adding your education, work experience, skills, and more.
  • Don't forget to ask for feedback from mentors and peers to improve your CV!

Want to have a look before you register? You can use the guest access option to explore the platform.

  • You can view CVs made by other users.
  • You can also get familiar with the platform and what it has to offer.
  • However, you won't be able to save or edit any CVs without registering. In addition, you won't be able to rate or provide feedback without an account.
